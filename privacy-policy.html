Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Game Data: We generate and store random usernames along with scores in a public ICloud database. Users have the option to modify their generated usernames.
  • Local Storage: We may store game data locally which is used only to improve performance. This data is not synced or uploaded to any online database.

External Services

Our application uses the following external service:

GrindFrench

  • No external services

WordBlast

  • Dictionary API: We use the Free Dictionary API (api.dictionaryapi.dev) to verify word validity. When you submit a word for verification, it is sent to this external service. No personal information is shared with this service.
